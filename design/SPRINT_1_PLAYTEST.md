# Sprint 1 Playtest

## Objective

Verify that the room itself communicates mounting chaos before the HUD is consulted.

## Test duration

Five minutes.

## Questions

1. Which station did you notice first when pressure increased?
2. Did clicking feel like a meaningful intervention or busywork?
3. Was any warning visually unclear?
4. Did the room feel calmer after delivery?
5. At what point did the rhythm become tiring?

## Success criteria

- the player can identify the highest-risk station without reading percentages
- failure feels recoverable rather than arbitrary
- clicking alternates with observation instead of becoming continuous tapping
- completing delivery produces an obvious emotional release
