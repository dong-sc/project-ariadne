# Human Factors

## Flow-state rhythm

The game alternates between observation, intervention, recovery, calm, and renewed escalation.

- immediate feedback: under 120 ms
- micro-risk: every 4–9 seconds
- meaningful trade-off: every 20–45 seconds
- major resolution: every 2–5 minutes

## Stress escalation

Stress must be visible before it becomes punitive:

1. neutral
2. yellow warning
3. animation or sound cue
4. slowdown
5. recoverable stage failure

## Ethical retention

The game should rely on mastery, insight, and incomplete patterns—not fake urgency, obscured odds, punishment for taking breaks, or unbounded tapping requirements.
