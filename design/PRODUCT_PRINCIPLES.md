# Product Principles

1. The room must feel alive before the menus feel complete.
2. Pressure must create choices, not helplessness.
3. Every click must have a visible causal result.
4. Better workflow must visibly reduce future clicking.
5. Routine rewards never block the player.
6. Learning is delivered through consequences, not lectures.
7. Dong Lin Photo appears as earned meaning, not an interruption.
8. Art and interface must share one visual grammar.
9. One excellent five-minute slice is worth more than fifty shallow systems.
10. The endgame fantasy is calm, not maximal noise.
11. Events may be unpredictable; their causes must remain understandable afterward.
12. Equipment changes what the workflow can absorb, not an abstract power score.

## Ariadne Principle

Every mechanic must reduce chaos or reveal it.
